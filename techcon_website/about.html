Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for technology enthusiasts and has since grown into an internationally recognized conference. Each year, we gather top minds from across the globe to explore the latest in innovation and digital transformation.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire, educate, and empower the global tech community by providing a platform for collaboration, learning, and cutting-edge thought leadership.

Notable Past Speakers

Jane Liu

Jane Liu, CTO of InnovateX, shared insights on ethical AI development in 2022. Her work in AI governance continues to influence the industry worldwide.

Dr. Ahmed Khan

Dr. Khan presented on quantum computing breakthroughs at TechCon 2021. As a leading researcher at Qubit Labs, his contributions have been pivotal in the field.

Sophia Martinez

Sophia, a cybersecurity strategist, discussed the future of digital safety at the 2023 conference. Her innovative defense strategies have been adopted by Fortune 500 companies.
